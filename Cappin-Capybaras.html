What is a NFT?

NFTs (Non Fungible Tokens) are a way to own somthing within the digital space. Once you purchase it, it becomes yours; you own the rights to that file. Our file format is art but NFTs can take form in many different ways. These can include: art, music, videos, or really anything that can be put on a digital device.

How do I mint?

Well, to start minting you'll want to connect your wallet. If you are unsure about how to do that, look athe the "How do I connect my wallet?" question below. After doing so you can scroll up and click the "Mint" button. It's as simple as that.

How much does it cost to mint?

The price to mint a Capy is 0.33 SOL (Solana).

How many are in the collection?

There is a total of 2,500 Capys within the collection. This includes the 13 1/1 and the randomly generated Capys.

When can I sell my Capy?

Look below at the Roadmap. We want to have you be able to sell/buy your Capys right after the mint process has finished.

How do I connect my wallet?

If you look on the top right of website (or within the menu bar for mobile users) you can see "Connect Wallet". Our currently accepted wallets include: Sollet and Phantom Wallet.
